We all love an onomatopoeia:

the formation of words whose sound is imitative of the sound of the noise or action designated

There are obvious ones like buzz and bang, but a few others you might not have considered before. Let us share with you a few of our favourite onomatopoeias in the English vocabulary:

1. Swish

2. Moaned

3. Twang

4. Gurgle

5. Quack

6. Murmur

7. Assonance

8. Click

9. Hiccup

10. Sizzle

11. Rustle
